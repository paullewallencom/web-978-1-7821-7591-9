Eastern Sierra

The Eastern Sierra, situated along US 395 on the eastern side of the Sierra Nevada mountain range, is still located in California. It is a paradise for photographers and hikers and somehow unexplored territory. The main part is in Mono and Inyo County and sometimes referred to as California's wild side. It contains the highest mountain, Mt. Whitney as well as the road to the lowest point: Badwater in Death Valley. Towns along US 395 include Lone Pine, famous location for Westerns, Bishop, Mammoth Lakes, June Lake, Lee Vining and the ghost town Bodie.
