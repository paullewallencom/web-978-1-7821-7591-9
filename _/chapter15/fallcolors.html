Fall Colors

In several areas of the Eastern Sierra you can find spectacular displays of fall colors. Glowing aspens, yellow to golden to red justify a special trip that part of the year. Unfortunately, it is hard to tell in advance when to go - it is only a two or three week window - and every year is different. Once weather with heavy winds hits the region all the leaves will blow away. It is best to check with folks that live there or to check some of the web sites.
