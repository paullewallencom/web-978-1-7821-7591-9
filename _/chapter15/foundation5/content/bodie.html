Bodie

Bodie is a ghost town turned into a State Park. Once it was a highly populated mining town. Then and today it is often the hottest spot in the state of California in the summer and the coldest in the winter

The town closed down for business in 1937 but today it looks like this happened only yesterday. With its great light, blue skies and well conserved buildings, it is a paradise for photographers.
