Lee Vining

Lee Vining might possibly be the one town in California that millions of people visit without an intention to do so. It is the first town you reach coming out of Yosemite National Park. Unprepared travellers will often look in vain for lodging.It's main attraction is nearby Mono Lake and its best kept secret is the Mobil Station. It is the best place to eat. Try the lobster tacquitos or the steak sandwich .
