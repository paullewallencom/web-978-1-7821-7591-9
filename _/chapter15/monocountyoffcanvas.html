Welcome to Mono County, the heart of the Eastern Sierra. Mono County is located along US395 in between Bishop and Lake Tahoe. It has famous Yosemite National Park on the westside and the state of Nevada on the east. It is a paradise for hikers and flyfishers.

June Lake

Once I discovered it, June Lake has always been my home away from home. I do not feel like retiring, but ever I have to , this is where it would be.

Tioga Pass

At over 9,900 ft., Tioga Pass is one of the highest crossings on a State Route in the country. It also marks the eastern entrance (or exit) of Yosemite National Park. During the few months a year you can actually drive to it, it is a marvelous place for hiking and photography. Tioga Pass opened one year on May 21st, July 2nd some other year.

Lee Vining

Lee Vining might possibly be the one town in California that millions of people visit without an intention to do so. It is the first town you reach coming out of Yosemite National Park. Unprepared travellers will often look in vain for lodging.It's main attraction is nearby Mono Lake and its best kept secret is the Mobil Station. It is the best place to eat. Try the lobster tacquitos or the steak sandwich

Mono Lake

The most unusual views in the Eastern Sierra you get to see as soon as you come down Tioga Pass towards Vining. Right before the junction with US 395 is a vista point overlooking Mono Lake. The water of most of the inlets of this lake go into the L.A. aqueduct. As a result the water level of this very alkaline lake is a lot lower than before, exposing the crystalline structures that developed below the lake surface, called tufas. Five miles south and then five miles east on SR 120 is the South Tufa area, the best spot for viewing and photography.

Bodie

Bodie, now a California State Park, was a big mining town with a population of around 10,000 in 1880. In the winter time, it competes with Truckee for the coldest place in California. It is a very interesting place to visit and, of course, to take pictures. The Boone Store, closed over 70 years ago looks like it did just last night. The 13 mile road from US 395 to Bodie (paved except for the last three miles) is situated between Bridgeport and Lee Vining.The picture on your left is that of, believe it or not, a hotel. Ever since I went there, it looked like if it was about to fall down. No, there are no vacancies.

Mammoth Lakes

Mammoth Lakes is one of the most popular ski resorts of California. In the summer time Mammoth Mountain transforms into a mountain biker paradise. The nearby Rainbow Falls and, freak of nature, Devil's Postpile are two must visits

June Lake fishing season

As is the tradition, the fishing season in June Lake has started on the last Sunday of April. Parts of some of the lakes were still frozen, but that did not prevent anybody from having fun and catching big fish

Tioga Pass open

Tioga Pass opened relatively early this year. On May 10 the gates at the east entrance of Yosemite National Park were already opened.

Lee Vining

With Tioga Pass open again, crowds are about to discover the Whoa Nellie deli at the Mobil Station again, at the junction of US395 and the Tioga Road.My favorite dishes there are the steak sandwich and the lobster tacquitos.

Bodie

Mammoth Lakes

Like every year, the Mammoth ski area transformed into a paradise for mountain bikers. People can go up the mountain with the gondola en bike back down using one of several popular trails, such as the popular Kamikaze trail.
