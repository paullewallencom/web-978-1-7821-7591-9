Rock Creek - Little Lakes Valley

From Tom's Place, north of Bishop, you can take the 9 mile road along Rock Creek that takes you to Mosquito Flat, a parking lot and trail head at 10,000ft. From there you can walk into the John Muir Wilderness. The trail takes you into Little Lakes Valley. It is one of the most beautiful walks in the Eastern Sierra.
