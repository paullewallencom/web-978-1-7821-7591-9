Tioga Pass

At over 9,900 ft., Tioga Pass is one of the highest crossings on a State Route in the country. It also marks the eastern entrance (or exit) of Yosemite National Park. During the few months a year you can actually drive to it, it is a marvelous place for hiking and photography. Tioga Pass opened one year on May 21st, July 2nd some other year.
