Saddlebag Lake - Twenty Lakes Area

Just east of Yosemite National Park is a two mile dirt road to Saddlebag Lake. A water taxi will take you to the other side of the lake, where 20 more lakes are waiting for your enjoyment, at an elevation of approximately 11,000 feet. Take it slowly and take a lot of pictures. The water taxi service usually starts two weeks after Tioga Pass opens and ends at the end of September. Click on any photograph for more details.
