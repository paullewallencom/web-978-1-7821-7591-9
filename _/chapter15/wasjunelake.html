June Lake Loop

The June Lake Loop begins just five miles south of Lee Vining, on US 395, 12 miles east of Tioga Pass. The 16 mile loop goes by four lakes and in the southern part you will discover the charming town of June Lake. In my opinion this is the gem of the entire Eastern Sierra.
